LONE RIDER RACE SERIES

Presented by Waco Run Club

​

Free to enter series of challenges and races through the Strava platform. The race series is most active during summer months, but challenges may pop up at any time. Some events will be abstract, while others will require you to race from one Waco landmark to another. It is open to anyone, whether a member of GTC or not, so invite your friends.

​

The race series will be conducted primarily through our Strava club page. Challenges will appear on occasion in the post section. You will then comment your result for the week's event or email it to us. Strava is a free to use social media platform for endurance athletes. A link to our club page can be found at the bottom of this website or in the contact info page.
